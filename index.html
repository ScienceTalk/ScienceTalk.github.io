Who we are

We are computer scientists with a strong interest in people and how they use technology. We are not affiliated in any way with any science policy or health organizations or special-interest groups. Please contact us at sciencechats@gmail.com or @science_chats if you have any questions or concerns.

What are we doing on Twitter?

We are experimenting with sending tweets to people who seem to have some interest in certain health and science topics. We send tweets from our twitter account @science_chats requesting people to participate in our study. We are very conservative with choosing whom to send a tweet. You should not receive a tweet from us if you did not respond to our first tweet or if you responded negatively. However we may still make mistakes, and we apologize if we sent you a tweet that did not make sense or offended you.

How does it Work

Upon receiving a tweet from us, if you choose to participate, you will have to complete a questionnaire. Your participation in this study is voluntary. You do not have to be in this study if you don't want to be. Moreover, ScienceChat takes your privacy very seriously. For a complete overview of our privacy policy please look at the Confidentiality link above.
